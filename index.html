Welcome to Singapore Airlines Virtual (ATC24), where realism is not just encouraged; it's our standard.

Inspired by the legacy and excellence of real-world Singapore Airlines, we operate under one guiding belief:

"A Great Way to Fly."

Our community reflects this philosophy through realistic pilot operations, authentic ATC phraseology, and structured training for all roles, including flight deck, cabin crew, and air traffic control. Every flight is purposeful, and every procedure matters.

We value professionalism, discipline, and teamwork, blending traditional aviation standards with a modern and engaging community. While mistakes are part of the learning process, cutting corners is not part of our culture.

Looking ahead, our ambition is clear. We are dedicated to growing and becoming a community of over 500 aviation enthusiasts, including passionate pilots and controllers, who share a commitment to realism and quality.

This isn't just a Discord server; it's a standard and a mindset.

It's A Great Way to Fly—virtually.

Fly smart. Fly realistic. Fly Singapore Airlines Virtual.
